#numbers Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 28, 2026

#numbers is a social app where users can participate using a unique number instead of a public name or profile photo. This policy explains what information the app handles and how it is used.

Information We Handle

The app may handle account information such as a generated user ID, unique number, phone verification details entered during onboarding, posts, comments, likes, follows, private messages, report submissions, verification requests, and media users choose to add or access through device permissions.

How Information Is Used

Information is used to provide core app functionality, including account creation, anonymous profile display, social posting, comments, messages, notifications, moderation, reporting, account safety, and verification features.

Device Permissions

#numbers may request camera, microphone, and photo or media access when a user chooses to use features that require those permissions. Users can control these permissions through their device settings.

Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Information may be processed by service providers that help operate, secure, moderate, or improve the app, when needed to provide the service or comply with legal obligations.

Safety and Moderation

Users can report content that appears to violate community standards. Reported content may be reviewed for moderation and safety purposes.

Children

#numbers is intended for users age 13 and older. Users must confirm they are at least 13 years old during onboarding.

Data Retention and Deletion

Information is retained as needed to provide the app, maintain safety, resolve issues, and comply with legal obligations. Users may contact us to request account or data deletion.
