#numbers Child Safety Standards

Effective date: June 28, 2026

#numbers does not allow child sexual abuse material (CSAM), child sexual exploitation, grooming, sexual solicitation of minors, or any content or behavior that endangers children.

Prohibited Content and Conduct

Users may not upload, post, request, distribute, promote, or link to CSAM, sexualized content involving minors, grooming behavior, child exploitation, trafficking, coercion, or attempts to move minors into unsafe conversations or services.

Reporting

Users can report content in the app when they believe it violates safety rules. Child safety concerns can also be sent directly to ingidrissakamara@gmail.com.

Review and Enforcement

Reported content may be reviewed and removed when it violates these standards. Accounts involved in child exploitation, grooming, or other severe safety violations may be suspended or removed.

Authority Reporting

Where required by applicable law, #numbers will report confirmed or suspected CSAM or child exploitation concerns to the appropriate regional or national authorities.

Designated Contact

The designated contact for child safety compliance is ingidrissakamara@gmail.com.
